About us

For over sixty years and three generations, Porta Door has proved to be a reliable source of fine woodworking components to both large and small woodworking professionals alike. We are proud of the standards we set to serve our customers. Specializing in Custom: Cabinet Doors & Drawer Fronts, Dovetail Drawer Boxes, Cabinet & Architectural Mouldings, Square & Beaded Face Frames, Wine Racks & Cabinet Accessories, Complete Finishing Cut To Size Lumber and much more.

Cabinet Doors
Cope and Sticks

The largest selection of cope and stick door styles in the industry produced to the highest standards
in all types of designs and hardwoods.

MT Constructions

Our MT construction allows traditional mould and tenon joinery for the most complex stil and rail profiles.

Miter Doors

Precision machined mitered joints provide the strongest and tightest joint
assemblies for a product that is second to none.

Applied Moulds

Applied mouldings add more depth and creativity to create a unique design.

Rope Doors

Rope designs can be added to all doors and drawer front designs, available in all wood types.

Louvre Doors

Open Louvres are available in 4/4 and 5/4 thickness' in all wood types -
non-see-through, allowing ventilation.

MDF Doors

MDF doors are a mixture of wood fibers, resin, and wax, which are pressed into flat panels
with our state of the art machines. These machines operate
at high pressures and temperatures to create the perfect density for your doors.

Specialty Doors

We can manufacture a wide variety of custom doors to your specifications and drawings.

Drawer Fronts

Slab and matching door design available with raised or flat panel applications.
