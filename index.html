Cope and Sticks

MT Constructions

Our MT construction allows traditional mould and tenon joinery for the most complex stil and rail profiles.

Miter Doors

Precision machined mitered joints provide the strongest and tightest joint
assemblies for a product that is second to none.

Applied Moulds

Applied mouldings add more depth and creativity to create a unique design.

Rope Doors

Rope designs can be added to all doors and drawer front designs, available in all wood types.

Louvre Doors

Open Louvres are available in 4/4 and 5/4 thickness' in all wood types -
non-see-through, allowing ventilation.

MDF Doors

MDF doors are a mixture of wood fibers, resin, and wax, which are pressed into flat panels
with our state of the art machines. These machines operate
at high pressures and temperatures to create the perfect density for your doors.

Mouldings
Crown Moulding

Crown molding is part of a large family of moldings which are designed to gracefully flare out to a finished top edge. Crown molding is generally used for capping walls, pilasters, and cabinets, and is used extensively in the creation of interior and exterior cornice assemblies and door and window hoods.

Casings

Of all types, custom designed and manufactured to your special needs.

Baseboard

We make our baseboards with state of the art technology to help you protect your walls from impacts and to add decorative features.

Chair Rails

We manufacture the highest quality chair rails to help protect the walls surface from chair backs - or you may use them simply as decoration.

Base Cap

We make it possible for you to add moulding to the top piece of a base moulding, which increases the decorative look. it’s sometimes used with baseboard; base cap may also be used as a panel moulding or multipurpose profile.

Solid Crowns

We make top of the line non-finger-jointed moulding, solid crowns.

Wainscot Beads

is a beaded interlocked moulding for cabinet and wall applications.

Plynths and Stools

Need a plynth to enhance the appearance of your door trim? Plynths are a decorative block of wood, thicker and wider than a door casing.
